I wanted to make sure that it is obvious by looking at the graph which publisher has the highest overall global sales from over the years. I chose to do it hoizontally as since there is a lot of data and some of the bars are quite long, it is the best way to display the information. Also since I have included the names of the publishers beside their bars, the writing is also horizonal so it is is more convenient when reading the names. I have also chosen to use one colour for all of the publishers, as this makes the bar chart look less clustered and chaotic. I have sorted the bar charts to go in descending order. This makes it unchallenging when trying to find the publisher which has the highest overall global sales.
